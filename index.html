Our Services

BIM & CAD Design


> 3D Modeling:
Creating detailed three-dimensional models of buildings or infrastructure.
> Markup Interpretation:
Utilizing skill in interpreting markups created with Bluebeam software on drawings. This can involve understanding and incorporating design changes, annotations, or feedback from various stakeholders.
> Collaborative Review:
Using Bluebeam for collaborative reviews, allowing different team members to comment, edit, and provide feedback on project documents.
> Document Control:
Implementing a unified document control system by integrating Bluebeam with BIM, managing revisions, and ensuring version control.
> As-Built Documentation:
Updating models to reflect the actual construction as a reference for future projects.

Architectural Design


> Conceptual Design:
Brainstorming and developing initial design ideas. Creating concept sketches and mood boards.
> Schematic Design:
Developing the overall design concept. Preliminary layouts and floor plans.
> Design Development:
Refining the chosen design concept. Detailed drawings, material selection, and specifications.
> 3D Modeling:
Creating detailed three-dimensional representations of the design. Virtual walkthroughs for client visualization.
> Construction Documents:
Preparing comprehensive drawings and specifications for construction. Ensuring compliance with building codes and regulations.
> Interior Design:
Planning and designing interior spaces. Selection of furniture, finishes, and lighting.

Structural Design


> Structural Analysis:
Conducting thorough analysis of loads and forces acting on structures. Determining structural stability and integrity.
> Foundation Design:
Designing foundations to support the entire structure. Considering soil conditions and load-bearing capacity.
> Steel and Concrete Design:
Developing detailed designs for steel and concrete structures. Ensuring compliance with industry standards and codes.
> Seismic and Wind Analysis:
Assessing structures for resilience against seismic and wind forces. Implementing measures to enhance structural safety.
> Reinforcement Detailing:
Creating detailed drawings specifying placement of reinforcement in concrete structures. Ensuring structural durability and safety.

MEP Design


> Mechanical Design:
Designing HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) systems. Ensuring optimal temperature control, air quality, and energy efficiency.
> Electrical Design:
Designing electrical systems for buildings. Including lighting, power distribution, and fire alarm systems.
> Plumbing Design:
Designing plumbing systems for water supply and drainage. Incorporating fixtures, pipes, and wastewater treatment.
> Coordination and Clash Detection:
Collaborating with other disciplines to avoid clashes in design. Utilizing BIM for efficient coordination. Plumbing and Electrical Load Calculations: Calculating loads to determine system capacities. Ensuring systems meet the demands of the building.
